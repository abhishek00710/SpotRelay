# SpotRelay Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

SpotRelay helps nearby drivers coordinate parking spot handoffs. The app uses location, profile, and notification data only to make the handoff experience work.

## Information We Collect

- Location data: SpotRelay uses your current location and saved parked location to show nearby handoffs, post a spot, claim a spot, and remind you when you return to your car.
- Profile data: If you add a display name or profile photo, SpotRelay stores it so other drivers in an active handoff can recognize who they are coordinating with.
- Handoff activity: SpotRelay stores spot status, claim status, completion state, successful handoffs, successful shares, and no-show counts to support reliability and trust features.
- Device notification data: SpotRelay stores push notification tokens and basic notification permission state so it can send handoff updates to your device.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- show parking handoffs near you;
- let drivers post, claim, cancel, and complete handoffs;
- send important push notifications about active handoffs;
- remember your parked location and optionally nudge you when you return to your car;
- show lightweight trust indicators such as share stars.

## Background Location

SpotRelay may use background location features to detect parked-car reminder moments. Background location is used only for parking-related reminders and handoff functionality.

## Tracking and Advertising

SpotRelay does not sell your personal information. SpotRelay does not use your data for third-party advertising tracking.

## Service Providers

SpotRelay uses Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Cloud Messaging, and Firebase Functions, to provide sign-in, realtime database, push notification, and backend notification functionality.

## Data Retention

Parking spot documents include a cleanup timestamp intended for automatic deletion after 24 hours. Some profile, trust, and notification-token data may remain while you continue using SpotRelay so the app can provide your profile and notification experience.

## Your Choices

You can disable location access, background location, motion access, or notifications in iOS Settings. Some SpotRelay features may not work without those permissions.

## Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact the SpotRelay owner through the support channel listed on the App Store product page.
